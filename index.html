About

We work with people who are addicted to using drugs by injecting, smoking or drinking. Our vision is to help them overcome their addiction and create a safe environment where they can get the required assistance.

How do we help

Our organization help uses to find themselves and deal with their addiction. They are thought that they should love themselves and that they are special and have a reason to live a long and healthy life.
